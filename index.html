What Is A Hackathon?
A hackathon is a thrilling event that brings people together to creatively tackle challenges and build exciting projects, all while having a blast with other hackers!

The GooseHacks Discord will be used for announcements, to connect with other hackers and also for the schedule. Stay tuned for updates throuhh Instagram and other social medias!
Frequently Asked Questions
How Big Can My Team Be?
We recommend team sizes of 1 to 4 members, ensuring a conducive environment for collaboration, creativity, and efficient project development.
Who Is Eligible To Participate?
Whether you're a coding enthusiast or someone who has never coded before, we encourage you to come hack with us. We even have a Best Beginner Hack prize!
Where Does The Hackathon Occur?
GooseHacks will be conducted in a virtual format, allowing participants from all around the world to join us from the comfort of their own homes!
Still have more questions?
Email us questions at goosehacked@gmail.com or send us a direct message on Instagram @goosehacks2023!
What if I can't Program?
GooseHacks welcomes hackerrs of all skill level. Even if you don't know how to code we encourage you to join since hackathons can be a great place to learn!
Do I have to Pay to Attend?
Entry to GooseHacks is completely free! We also have awesome free prizes for winners!
When can Hackers Register?
We anticipate in a few weeks hacker applications will be open! Make sure to follow the GooseHacks social medias to be the first to hear the exciting news!
